Connect with other users and find professional help.

## How to get support

* [Ask questions and discuss problems](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow/issues)
* [Find out about latest release](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow/releases)
* [Find out about latest features](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow/issues?q=label%3Anews%20)
* [Get commercial support](https://mayberg.se/support/)
* [Contact a human](https://datenstrom.se/contact/)

## How to contribute

* [Answer questions](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow/issues)
* [Create useful plugins](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow-plugins)
* [Create beautiful themes](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow-themes)
* [Create new translations](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow-plugins/tree/master/language)
* [Follow us on Twitter](https://twitter.com/datenstromse)

[Datenstrom Yellow is open source](https://github.com/datenstrom/yellow). Licensed under GPLv2 unless stated otherwise. At the heart of open source is the idea that we make better software together. Contribution and feedback are very welcome.
